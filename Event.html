Upcoming Events at Bean & Bloom

Join us to enjoy great coffee, live music, creative workshops, and community gatherings! Here’s what’s coming up:

  • Open Mic Night – Every Friday, 7 PM. Bring your talent and enjoy live performances.
  • Latte Art Workshop – 1st Sunday of each month. Learn how to craft beautiful latte art with our baristas.
  • Live Jazz Morning – Every Saturday, 10 AM–12 PM. Start your weekend with smooth jazz and fresh brews.
  • Holiday Specials – December 1–31. Seasonal drinks and treats to celebrate the festive season.
  • Book Club Meetup – 2nd Wednesday monthly, 6 PM. Chat about your favorite reads over coffee.
  • Local Art Showcase – Monthly rotating exhibits by local artists, opening night every 3rd Thursday, 7 PM.
  • Community Board Game Night – Last Friday of each month, 6 PM. Fun and games for all ages!
  • Spring Coffee Tasting – April 15, 4 PM. Sample new coffee blends and learn from our experts.

Stay tuned to our social media for surprise pop-up events and exclusive offers!
